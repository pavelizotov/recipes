Potato Rösti (10–13 portions)

Ingredients

  • 12 potatoes
  • Salt
  • A touch of hacoweiss powder

Method

  1. Grate potatoes and wash thoroughly.
  2. Squeeze out all liquid.
  3. Season lightly with salt.
  4. Vacuum seal tightly and press flat.
  5. Steam at 100°C for 35 minutes.
  6. Chill in ice water.
  7. Portion for service.

Red Pepper Purée

Ingredients

  • 6 red bell peppers
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • ½ garlic bulb
  • Vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Thyme, rosemary

Method

  1. Cut vegetables, marinate with oil, vinegar, herbs.
  2. Roast at 190°C for 25 minutes.
  3. Remove herbs.
  4. Blend smooth with olive oil.
  5. Season.
  6. Strain through chinois.

Celery Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 60 ml olive oil
  • 20 ml apple cider vinegar
  • 10 ml lemon juice
  • 5 ml Dijon mustard
  • 5 ml maple syrup
  • Salt, white pepper
  • 50 g fresh green celery

Method

  1. Whisk oil, vinegar, lemon, mustard, syrup.
  2. Add celery.
  3. Season.
